Word length statistics

Abstract

This article is about statistics of word length. It answers about the mean length of words in English and Russian, and correlation with the popularity of it. Also, I wrote about how did I write technical side of this article.

Statistics

On this chart, you can see the count of words, with a different length. Here are using 20k most popular words and 10k most popular words datasets. Using 10k dataset, most popular (and avarage) word length is 6 symbols. As we can see the difference between datasets, further is the word length from 6 symbols (longer), than it is less popular.

Also, there is the Russian language chart. Russian chart is similar but seems to be moved the right by 1 letter. The average Russian words are longer than English words.
