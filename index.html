Word length statistics in English

Abstract

This article is about statistics of word length in English. It answers about the mean length of words in English, and correlation with the popularity of it. Also, I wrote about how did I write technical side of this article.

Statistics

On this chart, you can see the count of words, with a different length. Here are using 20k most popular words and 10k most popular words datasets. Using 10k dataset, most popular (and mean) word length is 6 symbols. As we can see the difference between datasets, further is the word length from 6 symbols (longer), than it is less popular.
